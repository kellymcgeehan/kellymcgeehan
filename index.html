Kelly Ann McGeehan is a creative writer based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She is currently working on her BA and MA in English at Temple University.

She mostly writes poetry, but also does a bit of everything else. Her other interests include creative nonfiction, short stories, and photojournalism.

Her personal essay, "On Delays," is published in Hyphen Literary and Arts Magazine. The rest of her work is available to view here.

Kelly currently works part-time at the Philadelphia Inquirer and the Free Library of Philadelphia Foundation. After graduation, she hopes to write for a newspaper or literary magazine.

For inquiries, or just to say hello, you can reach Kelly at kelly.mcgeehan@temple.edu.
