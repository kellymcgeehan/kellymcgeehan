body keeps the score: erasure

poetry

the medical term for blank stares is

escape emotions roared into nothing.

i tell stories to keep alive to make sense

out of what is virtually useless.

easier to tell a story than feel

dread triggered by daily experience

ongoing i continue to

not know how to be a patient

successfully treated. what has happend

cannot be dealt with imprints of

the fog that robs you of

what i call the chapters to come.

-of your self this overwhelm shame

finding a way to remind the

past finding a way to keep

from yourself secrets about ways

to survive.

Annotations

The piece selected here is a unpublished poem titled “body keeps the score: erasure” that I wrote for my poetry class. I am showcasing this piece because I want my portfolio to communicate that I am a promising creative writer, and I believe my poetry best exemplifies this potential.
